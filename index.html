FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, reach out on WhatsApp.

What's the difference between own name and UBO service? ▼

With own name registration, you appear as the director and shareholder on all public filings. With a UBO service, a professional is listed instead, keeping your details private while you retain full control.

What is the managed UBO service? ▼

Our managed UBO service handles the beneficial ownership structuring for your company. We take care of compliance and registration so you can focus on your business. The best structure depends on your needs and target market — we'll help you choose during consultation.

How long does incorporation take? ▼

Most setups complete within 7 business days once we have all your documents. Some jurisdictions may take slightly longer depending on government processing.

Do I need to travel anywhere? ▼

No. The entire process is 100% remote. Incorporate your company from anywhere in the world.

Can I open a bank account? ▼

Yes. We guide you through the bank account process and recommend the best options for your jurisdiction and business type.

Is this suitable for ecommerce / dropshipping? ▼

Absolutely. The majority of our clients are ecommerce entrepreneurs and dropshippers. Our services are built specifically for online businesses.
